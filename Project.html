Snow Heating Pad: (Software, Hands-On Electrical, 3D CAD)

Ottawa is most affected by the snow is the school's students and staff. They needed a controllable heating mat that could melt snow by using internal heaters to increase the surface temperature of the mat. The device is very easy to disassemble, with a built-in Bluetooth sensor and temperature sensor, users can remotely control the heating pad to fully utilize its functions and save power. Often the most effective way to solve a problem is to take inspiration from what works. The proposed device is designed as a modular tile to provide selective and adaptive heat.
